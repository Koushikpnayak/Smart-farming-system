Northern Leaf Blight

Detected in: Maize · Caused by fungus Exserohilum turcicum

AI Confidence94%

💊 Recommended Treatment

  • Apply Mancozeb 75% WP @ 2g/L water as foliar spray immediately
  • Spray Propiconazole 25% EC @ 1ml/L after 7 days if severity persists
  • Remove and destroy heavily infected leaves to reduce spread
  • Avoid overhead irrigation — switch to drip or furrow

🛡 Prevention Tips

  • Use resistant hybrid varieties like DKC 9081 in future seasons
  • Maintain crop rotation with non-host crops (soybean, cotton)
  • Apply preventive fungicide at 8-leaf stage in high-risk seasons
